Service details

QA-Notification is a web service that offers a mechanism to store and retrieve notifications tickets.
  • ApplicationId:
    • Internal code for authorizated application client. Only authorizated clients can store and retrieve notifications tickets.
  • Client_id:
    • Client identifier for MEMENTO.
  • ManagedMerchantId:
    • Merchant identification for PCI-BOX. It's an identifier of a registed shop in PCI-BOX.
    • Lenght must be 37 character exactly.
    • It's formed by: 'M' + Eight hexadecimal digits at lower case + '-' + Four hexadecimal digits at lower case + '-' + Four hexadecimal digits at lower case + '-' + Four hexadecimal digits at lower case + '-' + Twelve hexadecimal digits at lower case. Example: M01ab23cd-fe01-1fe9-0001-ff98ed87dc76
  • Merchant_id:
    • Merchant identification for MEMENTO. It's an identifier of a registed shop in MEMENTO.
    • Alphanumeric characters. No limit.
  • TransactionId:
    • Transaction identifier of operation asociated.
    • Length must be between 6 to 255 character.
  • Date:
    • Custom format timestamp for creating notifications with a specified date.
    • Length must be 17 character exactly with the following format: yyyyMMddhhmmssmil
      • yyyy: year from 1900 to 2099. Example: 2015.
      • MM: month from 00 to 12. Example: 03.
      • dd: day from 01 to 31. Example: 11.
      • hh: hour from 00 to 24. Example: 21.
      • mm: minutes from 00 to 59. Example: 57.
      • ss: seconds from 00 to 59. Example: 12.
      • mil: milliseconds from 000 to 999. Example: 217.
  • BeginDate:
    • Custom format timestamp for begin in filtering notifications.
    • Length must be 17 character exactly with the following format: yyyyMMddhhmmssmil
      • yyyy: year from 1900 to 2099. Example: 2015.
      • MM: month from 00 to 12. Example: 03.
      • dd: day from 01 to 31. Example: 11.
      • hh: hour from 00 to 24. Example: 21.
      • mm: minutes from 00 to 59. Example: 57.
      • ss: seconds from 00 to 59. Example: 12.
      • mil: milliseconds from 000 to 999. Example: 217.
  • EndDate:
    • Custom format timestamp for ending in filtering notifications.
    • Length must be 17 character exactly with the following format: yyyyMMddhhmmssmil
      • yyyy: year from 1900 to 2099. Example: 2015.
      • MM: month from 00 to 12. Example: 03.
      • dd: day from 01 to 31. Example: 11.
      • hh: hour from 00 to 24. Example: 21.
      • mm: minutes from 00 to 59. Example: 57.
      • ss: seconds from 00 to 59. Example: 12.
      • mil: milliseconds from 000 to 999. Example: 217.
